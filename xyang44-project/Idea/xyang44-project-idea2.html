Green bean cake

What is Green bean cake

Green bean cake is the famous dessert in China, but it has differnt types in differenty city. Each city has their famous brand of Green bean cake. But the people in Wuhan, China think their Green bean cake is the most delicious, expecially Cao Xiang Tai. The CXT was created by Cao Nanshan in 1884. After many years, it becomes the famous brand in Wuhan, China.

How to make Green bean cake

The materials
Green bean; sesame oil; white granulated sugar; and malt sygar.
