Chinese desserts!

Bow Ties

Children will love these egg roll wrappers, which are twisted and deep-fried, dipped into a brown sugar syrup, and left to harden. Children love it. For a more "adult friendly" version, prepare and deep-fry the bow ties, then lightly dust with sugar.

The materials
1 package egg; 4 to 6 cups oil; 1 cup brown sugar; corn syrup; honey; water.

Make it!

Green bean cake

Green bean cake is the famous dessert in China, but it has differnt types in differenty city. Each city has their famous brand of Green bean cake. The CXT was created by Cao in 1884. It becomes the famous brand in Wuhan, China.

The materials
Green bean; sesame oil; white granulated sugar; and malt sygar.

Steamed Sponge Cake

What makes this Chinese steamed sponge cake different from a typical sponge cake is that it is steamed, making it extremely moist. Closer to a Western idea of dessert is this soft, springy sponge cake made simply with eggs, sugar and flour.

The materials
eggs; white sugar; water; cake flour; baking powder.
